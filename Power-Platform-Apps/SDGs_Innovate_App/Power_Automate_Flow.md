The app had some functionality which were triggered with the help of Power Automate flow.

Below are the flows created
1. SpeakUp email flow: this flow enables female users who escalated gender-based unfair treatment to receieve an automated email to enable them know that their complaints has been logged and provide assurance of it been treated.
